# MessageYou - Visual-First Civic Platform

## Startup Idea Summary
MessageYou is a universal, visual-first platform where users can snap a photo (of a person, car, place, item, shop, etc.) to start or join a topic. AI maps the image to a real-world entity—enabling public discussion, community reports, and civic history. Everything from scam alerts to kind deeds can now be documented, linked, and tracked. No need to know names, platforms, or tags—just point, post, and connect.

## Problem We Solve
- No central place to track people, businesses, or locations when problems or notable actions arise
- Scams, crimes, kind acts, or lost items are lost in private messages or closed platforms
- Users can't reach people/places without contacts or social handles
- Businesses with fake promos, shady practices, or bad service are hard to flag at scale
- No way to search reputation/history of an unknown face, plate, or place
- Missed opportunity for a persistent, civic reputation layer linked to the real world

## Our Solution
- **Snap a photo** → AI maps it to a known (or new) real-world entity
- **Automatically surfaces** existing reports, reviews, alerts, and threads
- **Anyone can post or reply**: report scams, praise good acts, warn others, or join discussions
- **AI organizes** all reports into unified "blocks" for each entity, preserving original posts but filtering highlights
- **Works for everything visual**: people, cars, businesses, places, events, products
- **Detect repeat patterns**: scammers, fake companies, bad drivers, lost items, and more
- **Business and civic reputation** becomes open, searchable, and crowd-driven

## Target Audience
- Everyday users wanting to report or check on people, places, or things
- Victims of scams or civic incidents
- Local communities needing transparency and open reporting
- Journalists, watchdogs, and concerned citizens
- Small businesses and shops wanting to respond or claim their entity
- City and event organizers needing public feedback

## Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js v15.3.2
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4
- **Animations**: GSAP with ScrollTrigger
- **3D Graphics**: Three.js
- **2D Demos**: Phaser 3
- **Backend Simulation**: JSON + localStorage + cookies
- **Design**: Futuristic AI-inspired interface

## Getting Started

```bash
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) to see the MVP.

## MVP Features
- Camera/photo input → AI maps to entity or starts new one
- Public, anonymous post threads per entity
- Basic AI filtering (spam, hate, duplicates)
- Search by face, plate, location, keyword
- Post tags: good/bad, lost/found, scam, review
- Basic voting & report system

## Mission
Empower citizens to speak out, share real-world truth, and protect each other. Build a trusted layer of public memory—so good actions shine, and bad actors can't hide.

**Tagline**: "Snap. Post. Expose. Remember."
